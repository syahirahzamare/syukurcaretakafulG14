Family (Life) Takaful

Family Takaful is a long-term, Shariah-compliant plan designed to provide financial protection and savings for your family. It works on the concept of mutual assistance, where contributions are shared to help members facing hardship.

Who is it for?

Ideal for heads of households and primary income earners.

What does it cover?

  • Death Benefit – Provides a lump-sum payment to beneficiaries.
  • Total & Permanent Disability (TPD) – Financial support if unable to work.

Based on Islamic values

  • Tabarru’ (Donation) – Part of the contribution is donated to a shared fund.
  • Mutual Assistance – Members support one another.

Your family’s financial stability remains protected even in uncertain times.

Medical Takaful

Medical Takaful provides Shariah-compliant healthcare protection to help participants manage medical and hospitalization costs.

What does it cover?

  • Hospitalization & Surgery – Covers room charges, surgery, and treatment costs.
  • Outpatient Treatment – Includes selected treatments such as dialysis and cancer care.

Why it matters

  • Protects savings from unexpected medical expenses.
  • Provides ethical and Shariah-compliant healthcare coverage.

    Hibah Takaful

    Hibah Takaful allows participants to allocate Takaful benefits as a gift to their chosen beneficiaries, ensuring immediate financial support.

    Why choose Hibah Takaful?

    • Immediate financial assistance – Benefits are paid directly to nominees.
    • Avoids lengthy inheritance process – Funds do not go through probate.
    • Reduces disputes – The hibah cannot be contested.
